Design 23 ......
is an intimate and creative jewelry design studio located in Northern California. Just outside of San Francisco, in the redwood hills above the town of Woodside, the visual art of jewelry is perfected.
Using various materials ...... freshwater pearls, Swarovski crystals, hand-blown glass beads, semi-precious gemstones and other unique beads & materials, many beautiful pieces of jewelry are designed and created for each fashion season.
The jewelry of Design 23 reflects not only the latest in fashion trends and colors but is designed to maintain a strong sense of style and individuality.
